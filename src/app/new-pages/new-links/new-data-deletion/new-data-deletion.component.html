Data Deletion Information

Data Deletion Information for Fifibuy, Inc. and Affiliates (FiBeiGreetings, FiBeiDigiCards, etc.)

At Fifibuy, Inc., we are committed to protecting the privacy and data security of our users. In compliance with global data privacy regulations, including GDPR and CCPA, we provide you with the ability to request the deletion of your personal data from our systems. This policy applies to all services operated under Fifibuy, Inc., including but not limited to FiBeiGreetings and FiBeiDigiCards.

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

To initiate a data deletion request, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit a Request: You can request data deletion by contacting us at {{'fifibuymarketing@gmail.com'}} or through the “Account Settings” section of your profile on our website or app.
  2. Provide Account Information: Include your account details such as the registered email, username, and any relevant information to help us identify your account.
  3. Specify Scope of Deletion: Please indicate whether you wish to delete specific information (e.g., email, purchase history) or request the deletion of your entire account and associated data.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

Upon receiving your request, the following personal data will be deleted:

  • Personal Information (name, email address, contact details)
  • Account details (profile, preferences)
  • Transaction history and order details
  • Communication records (messages, notifications)
  • Data related to product usage (purchases, downloads)

Exceptions:

In some instances, we may be required to retain certain data for legal, regulatory, or business continuity purposes. This may include:

  • Records necessary to comply with tax or financial regulations
  • Data required for dispute resolution or ongoing investigations
  • Information retained for security or fraud prevention

Timeframe for Deletion:

We aim to process all data deletion requests within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation once the process has been completed.

Third-Party Data:

If your data has been shared with third-party partners (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners), we will notify these entities of your data deletion request. Please note that each partner may have its own data retention policies.

For any additional questions or concerns, feel free to contact our Data Privacy team at {{'fifibuymarketing@gmail.com'}}.
